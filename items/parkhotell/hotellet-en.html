Inexpensive living in the centre of Luleå
Our business idea is to offer inexpensive living in a quiet environment, in the centre of Luleå. We accommodate most requests, for business, families, conference groups and others. Our guests value low prices, a high level of service in favor of expensive offers like luxury dinners, cocktail bars or night clubs. There is plenty of that within walking distance of the hotel without it affecting the room price.

In cooperation with Södra Hamn Restaurangen(5 minute walk) you are offered 10% discount on a dinner of your choice, every day of the week.

We, Ingela and Stefan, welcome you!
