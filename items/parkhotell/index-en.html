A family hotel in the centre of Luleå
When you're staying with us, entertainment, shopping and commuting options are within walking distance of the hotel. The city has a lot to offer: An intense out life, cafés and restaurants with tasteful, local food, archipelago excursions or the serenity of the countryside.

You can also watch sports on the elite level, Luleå hockey, Plannja and Luleå basket are among the best of their respective sports. Our business idea is to offer inexpensive living, in a quiet location, in the centre of Luleå.

We accommodate most patrons, for business, families, conference groups and others.
