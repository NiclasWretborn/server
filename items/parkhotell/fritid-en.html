Experience Luleå!
Luleå Archipelago
We have the worlds only brackish water archipelago where you can experience long shallow beaches, perfect for sun bathing, or boat trips to the islands. Bright summer nights spent having a sauna or fishing. If you visit us in winter or spring, you can ride snowmobiles or go cross country skiing in a nature that will dwell in your mind forever.
Kulturens hus
Kulturens hus(house of culture) is found in the centre of Luleå, by the north harbour, one minute walk from the hotel. The size of it is 14000 m² and houses the city library, concert hall, multi functioning foajées, tourist info, café and restaurant.
On the top floor Norrbottensmusiken is housed.

A city of sports
In Luleå there are plenty of high level sports events. We have, among others, Luleå Hockey, Plannja, Luleå Basket and IFK Luleå.
All of them famous trademarks for Luleå and Norrbotten. Tickets can be booked through us.

Shopping in Luleå
In Luleå centre there are plenty of things to see and do. Shopping in exciting stores, atmospheric restaurants and cafées. In Luleå HomeCenter in Notviken you will find everything you need for your home, garden or car. At storheden a few kilometers outside of town are many of Luleås shopping malls. In the city centre there are many big shopping malls.
