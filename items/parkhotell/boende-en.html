Comfortable living when you are visiting Luleå!
The rooms are newly renovated and all 14 rooms in the hotel have their own shower and toilet.

Breakfast is included in the price
Start the day in a calm environment with our breakfast buffé. On other hours you are treated to coffee, tea, hot chocolate with cookies, if you wish

Our rooms
All rooms have a 32” flat tv and WiFi. All rooms, including the hotel, are smoke free. Smokers are advised to smoke outside. Due to allergies, pets are not allowed in the hotel rooms. Our beds are made with satin sheets, and thanks to our cooperation with Textilservice, Sultanmadrasser, Höjekuddar and Kronborgstäcken, we know you will sleep well in our hotel.

Kitchen
If you want to cook your own food at the hotel, there is a public kitchen that accommodates this. The kitchen is fully equipped with stove, microwave, oven and other household supplies. Guests clean the kitchen after use

Free parking
Free parking with engine heaters are offered to all our guests.
